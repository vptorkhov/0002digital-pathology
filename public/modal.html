Cookie Policy

Flowwow uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, protect and improve our platform. This policy describes the ways and why we use these technologies, and the choices you have about their use.

Cookie is a small data file that is sent to your device. For example, a cookie may allow us to identify your browser while another may store your settings. There are two types of cookies that are used on the Flowwow Platform: session cookies and persistent cookies. The session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device after you close your browser and may be used the next time you access the services Flowwow.

We also use other technologies with similar cookie functionality, such as web beacons, pixels, mobile identifiers, and tracking URLS to retrieve log data. For example, our emails may contain web beacons and tracking URLS to determine whether you have opened a particular message or accessed a particular link.

Why Flowwow uses these technologies

We use these technologies in order to:

• гProvide you with the opportunity to use Flowwow services
• To enable, facilitate and simplify the operation and your access to the Flowwow services
• Better understand how you navigate and interact with Flowwow services, and improve Flowwow platform
• Show you the content that is most appropriate for you
• To monitor and analyze the operation and effectiveness of the services Flowwow
• Identify and prevent possible fraud

How to regulate the use of “cookies”

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your browser’s settings to refuse cookies by going to the Help section on your
browser’s toolbar.
